FieldPress Download

FieldPress is a WordPress plugin I helped develop at the University of British Columbia. It is an open educational resource.  When you install it on a WordPress website it creates a full-featured  field trip management platform that allows instructors to manage student assessment and present content as well as allowing student collaboration.It is still in beta but we have decided to make official releases available to the public.  More information and support can be found at
